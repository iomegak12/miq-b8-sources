About Agentic RAG

Intelligent Information Retrieval at Your Fingertips

What is Agentic RAG?

Agentic RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is an advanced AI-powered system that combines the power of large language models with intelligent information retrieval. Our system automatically selects and uses the most appropriate tools to answer your questions accurately and comprehensively.

How It Works

When you ask a question, our AI agent analyzes your query and automatically determines which information sources will provide the best answer. It can:

  • Search the Web: Get real-time information using Google Search
  • Access Wikipedia: Retrieve encyclopedic knowledge on any topic
  • Query ArXiv: Find relevant academic and research papers
  • Search Documentation: Access specialized technical documentation

The agent intelligently combines information from multiple sources to provide you with comprehensive, well-researched answers.
